unhosted web apps

freedom from web 2.0's hostage platforms

By "unhosted web apps" we mean browser-based apps with no server-side backend. Unlike server-side or client-server apps, unhosted web apps leave users in control of their valuable user data and privacy, by default.

Unhosted web apps are also more resilient and more scalable. Most importantly, unlike most other app platforms, the web is open and free: owned by the commons of all humankind.
