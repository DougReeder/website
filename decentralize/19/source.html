The internet

If you have a new computer which you would like to connect to the internet, then the first thing you need is an Autonomous System Number (ASN), at least one BGP peer, a network connection (probably fiber) between you and that peer, and at least one block of internet IP addresses. You can then announce your block to your peer, and start sending and receiving traffic.
